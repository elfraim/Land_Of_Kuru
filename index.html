Welcome to Land of Kuru
You are able to fight monsters, buy new weapons, gain stats, complete quests, heal yourself at the medic, gain gold from monsters, risk losing gold if you die and use the bank to safeguard your gold. You can play 3 classes, Druid, Warrior, Ranger, each has its own unique stats. 4 Different races each with they're own unique stats. Your goal is to reach level 20 without running out of gold and being unable to heal; Monsters get stronger the stronger you get.
